Zhiyong Wang

I am currently a third-year Ph.D. candidate in the Department of Computer Science & Engineering , The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK), fortunate to be advised by Prof. John C.S. Lui (ACM/IEEE fellow). Before joining CUHK, I obtained my bachelor's degree from the Advanced Class in Mathematics and Physics for Information Science at Huazhong University of Science and Technology (HUST) in 2021. From June 2023 to August 2023, I interned at MSR Asia Theory Center, where I fortunately worked with Dr. Wei Chen (IEEE fellow, Director of MSR Asia Theory Center) and Dr. Siwei Wang. I also feel very lucky to cooperate closely with many other excellent researchers, including Prof. Shuai Li from Shanghai Jiao Tong University, Prof. Dongruo Zhou from Indiana University, and Dr. Tong Yu from Adobe Research.

I am interested in multi-armed bandits and reinforcement learning theory. If you share common interests and would like to explore collaboration or simply have a discussion, feel free to contact me.
